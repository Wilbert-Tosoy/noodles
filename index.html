Authentic Filipino Noodle Dishes

The word “pancit” colloquially refers to noodles and noodle dishes. The actual word may have been derived from a Chinese phrase that sounds phonetically similar, which means “to cook conveniently.”
Like many Asian countries, the Philippines has a vast and proud noodle culture, and there are dozens upon dozens of noodle dishes served throughout the country.

A trip isn’t complete without feasting on the local cuisine.
